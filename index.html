My Message

Hey my Valentine,

The day I met you, I was so suspicious. I doubted you a lot. I made many mistakes. I pressured you to share pictures because I didn’t know whether to believe or not. Looking back, I realize how unfair that was.

From that day until now, we’ve had so many fights… and so many beautiful memories too. Late-night talks, endless messages, silly jokes — I’ll never forget those. You are the first girl with whom I created so many memories, even if they were virtual.

For the first 5–6 months, I always had doubts in some corner of my mind. I wasn’t fully open. But once we both opened up, we truly got to know each other deeply. Not in any wrong way — just understanding each other’s thoughts, fears, and emotions.

You stood by me in both good and bad times. I never gave you surprises or gifts, and you never expected them. That’s the kind of person you are — simple, genuine, and rare. In a world full of fake people, I met someone truly real.

I blamed you many times. I hurt you. Sometimes you cried because of me. I teased you thinking it was funny, without realizing how it affected you. For all of that, I’m truly sorry.

I don’t know if I was the best boyfriend. Honestly, I know I wasn’t. But I hope I gave you at least one wonderful year filled with moments worth remembering.

There’s so much more I want to say, but not everything can be put into words. I just hope you understand.

You were an important part of my life, and you always will be in some way. Thank you for teaching me so much — about love, about patience, about myself. In many ways, you were my therapist too.

I truly hope you have a beautiful, peaceful, and prosperous life ahead.

Thank you… my Valentine. ❤️
